Go to the <Link to="https://app.fildomains.com"><b>FNS Manager App</b></Link> and connect with your wallet. Search for the name you would like to renew and click on it to view its profile page. Click the `Extend` button.

<BrowserWindow url="https://app.fildomains.com" bg="#f3f4f7" img="/img/howto/v3-renew-names-1.png"/>

:::info
It is possible to renew a name that the connected wallet does not own or manage. Doing so does not make that wallet the owner of the name.
:::

Pick the number of years you would like to extend the name’s registration. Click “Next”, then “Open Wallet” to confirm the transaction.
<BrowserWindow url="https://app.fildomains.com" bg="#f3f4f7" padding="25px" img="/img/howto/v3-renew-names-2.png"/>

You can also renew multiple names at the same time. Click on “My Names”. Next, click on the multi-select checkmark button. Click on each name you would like to renew then click `Extend`
<BrowserWindow url="https://app.fildomains.com" bg="#f3f4f7" padding="25px" img="/img/howto/v3-renew-names-3.png"/>

Review the names you want to extend, then click “Next”.
<BrowserWindow url="https://app.fildomains.com" bg="#f3f4f7" padding="25px" img="/img/howto/v3-renew-names-4.png"/>

Pick the number of years you would like to extend the names for and click “Next”.
<BrowserWindow url="https://app.fildomains.com" bg="#f3f4f7" padding="25px" img="/img/howto/v3-renew-names-5.png"/>

Click “Open Wallet” to confirm the transaction.
<BrowserWindow url="https://app.fildomains.com" bg="#f3f4f7" padding="25px" img="/img/howto/v3-renew-names-6.png"/>
